Cookie Popup Test

This page tests the new Finnish cookie popup. The popup should appear automatically when the page loads (if no consent is stored).

Expected Behavior:

  • Popup appears centered on screen with blur overlay
  • Finnish text: "BloomLead käyttää evästeitä..."
  • Shows all cookie categories with individual toggles
  • Three buttons: "Hyväksy kaikki", "Hylkää ei-välttämättömät", "Tallenna valinnat"
  • Users can customize which cookies to accept
  • Necessary cookies are always enabled (disabled toggle)
  • Website is blocked until choice is made
  • Popup cannot be dismissed without making a choice
