## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and enabling essential website functions.

## Your Cookie Choices

### Managing Your Preferences

You have full control over which cookies we use:

1. **Cookie Banner**: Choose your preferences when you first visit
2. **Cookie Settings**: Change your mind anytime by clicking the cookie settings link
3. **Browser Settings**: Block all cookies through your browser (may affect functionality)

### What Happens When You Say No

- **Necessary Cookies**: Always active (required for website to work)
- **Functional Cookies OFF**: 
  - Videos start from beginning each time
  - Volume/speed reset to default
  - No "Continue Watching" feature
  - Language resets to default
- **Analytics Cookies OFF**: 
  - We can't improve the website based on usage
  - No impact on your experience
- **Marketing Cookies OFF**: 
  - You may see less relevant ads elsewhere
  - No social media integration
  - No impact on our website functionality

## Data Security

We protect your cookie data with:
- Secure transmission (HTTPS encryption)
- Limited data collection (only what's necessary)
- Automatic deletion when you withdraw consent
- No storage of personal information in cookies

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you can:

- **Access**: See what cookie data we have about you
- **Delete**: Remove all your cookie data

- **Withdraw**: Change your cookie consent anytime
