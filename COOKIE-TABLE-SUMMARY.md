# Cookie Summary Table
## BloomLead Webinar Platform

## Strictly Necessary Cookies (Always Active)

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Data Collected | Duration | Third Party |
|-------------|---------|----------------|----------|-------------|
| `webinar_session` | Session management | Random session ID | Session only | No |
| `x-csrf-token` | Security protection | Security token | 24 hours | No |
| `cookie_consent_status` | Remember consent choice | Consent status (accepted/rejected) | 1 year | No |
| `bloomlead_cookie_consent` | Detailed consent preferences | Cookie category choices + timestamp | 1 year | No |

## Your Control Over Cookies

| Action | How To Do It | What Happens |
|--------|--------------|--------------|
| **Accept All** | Click "Accept All" in cookie banner | All cookies enabled, full functionality |
| **Reject All** | Click "Reject All" in cookie banner | Only necessary cookies, basic functionality |
| **Customize** | Click "Cookie Settings" | Choose which types you want |
| **Change Later** | Use cookie settings link on any page | Update your preferences anytime |
| **Browser Block** | Browser privacy settings | May break website functionality |
